Welcome to Arduino Cape Town!

We are a group of people of all sorts of ages and different backgrounds who design and create things from electronics.

About Us

Although we use the name Arduino we also use other boards like the ZArduino and build all kinds of cool gizmos that do not always involve the Arduino. The main interaction takes place online on our Facebook group where members write about what they are currently working, ask for help on problems or just post stuff that they have discovered. It all started when a few hackers met at the Science Hack Day Cape Town in January 2012, and we decided to start an Arduino group; the rest is history.
